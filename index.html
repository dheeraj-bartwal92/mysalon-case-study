Project Overview

Bridging the gap between beauty
professionals and their clients

Dual User System

Separate tailored experiences for beauty clients seeking services and professionals managing their business, each with role-specific login flows and dashboards.

Smart Booking

Intuitive 3-step booking flow with service selection, calendar-based date/time picking, and instant confirmation with appointment management.

The Challenge

Understanding the problem space

The beauty and wellness industry faces a fragmented booking experience. Clients struggle to discover quality professionals, compare services, and manage appointments efficiently. Meanwhile, beauty professionals lack a unified platform to showcase their work, manage their calendar, and communicate with clients.

72%
of beauty clients prefer online booking over phone calls
45%
of appointments are missed due to poor communication
3.2x
more bookings when professionals have an online presence
01

Discovery Gap

Clients have difficulty finding qualified beauty professionals near them, relying primarily on word-of-mouth and social media.

02

Booking Friction

The traditional phone-based booking process leads to missed calls, double-bookings, and scheduling conflicts.

03

No-Show Problem

Without automated reminders and easy rescheduling, appointment no-shows cost professionals significant revenue.

04

Fragmented Tools

Professionals juggle multiple apps for booking, messaging, and client management, leading to inefficiency.

Our Solution

A unified platform for beauty
professionals and their clients

01

Research & Discovery

Conducted stakeholder interviews with 15+ beauty professionals and surveyed 200+ potential clients to understand pain points, workflows, and booking preferences across the beauty industry.

02

UX Architecture

Designed information architecture supporting dual user roles (clients and vendors) with distinct login experiences, tailored dashboards, and role-specific navigation flows.

03

UI Design System

Developed a warm, inviting design system using rose gold, burgundy, and cream tones that evoke luxury and approachability, with consistent typography, spacing, and component patterns.

04

Cross-Platform Development

Built using React Native with Expo, enabling a single codebase to deploy across iOS, Android, and Web, reducing development time by 60% while maintaining native-quality performance.

Key Features

Designed for delight,
built for performance

Onboarding

Guided First-Time Experience

A 3-step onboarding flow introduces the app's core value propositions with beautiful illustrations and clear messaging. Users choose their account type (client or professional) and are routed to the appropriate experience.

  • 3-step visual walkthrough
  • Account type selection (Client / Professional)
  • Social login options (Google, Apple, Biometric)
  • Skip option for returning users
Dual Login System

Separate Client & Vendor Portals

Unlike competitors that use a single login, MySalon provides visually distinct login experiences. The client login uses a warm, inviting cream theme, while the vendor portal features a sophisticated dark theme with business-specific fields.

  • Cream-themed client login with social auth
  • Dark-themed vendor portal with business ID field
  • Role-based routing and personalized dashboards
  • Seamless switching between login modes
Booking Engine

3-Step Booking Flow

A streamlined booking process that reduces friction and increases conversion. Users select a service, pick their preferred date and time slot, then confirm with a summary review before placing their appointment.

  • Service selection with pricing and duration
  • Visual calendar with available time slots
  • Booking confirmation with full summary
  • Appointment management (reschedule, cancel)
Communication

In-App Messaging

Real-time messaging enables direct communication between clients and beauty professionals. Discuss service details, share inspiration photos, coordinate schedules, and receive appointment reminders all within the app.

  • Real-time chat with read receipts
  • Message thread management
  • Push notification support
  • Unread message badges
